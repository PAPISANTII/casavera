About us

What is Casa Vera?

Casa Vera is a student residence designed specifically for university and Erasmus students coming to study in Cartagena. All our rooms are fully equipped and ready for you to move in from day one.

We are located in the heart of Cartagena, just 12 minutes on foot from UPCT and less than 1 minute from a direct bus stop to UCAM. Everything you need to focus on what really matters: your university experience.
