Getting here

Well connected to the whole city and to both universities

🚌

City bus

The Paseo Delicias stop is a 1-minute walk away. Lines 5, 7, 9, 14 and 18 (among others) run from here, with direct connections to the city centre and university campuses.

The Universities Line connects the harbour with UPCT and UCAM every 30 minutes, Monday to Friday during term time.

🚉

Coach and train

The Cartagena Bus Station is just 8 minutes on foot, with connections to Murcia, Madrid, Valencia and the rest of the region.

Renfe Cartagena train station is also less than 15 minutes away on foot.

🚗

By car

Direct access from the Mediterranean Motorway (A-30). Public parking is available near Paseo de las Delicias and the harbour.

🚶

On foot or by bike

The historic centre, market, supermarkets and the university area are all within easy walking distance. Cartagena is a compact city — perfect for getting around without a car.
